How To Play

Neon Feud is a party game based on the bizarre, unhinged search queries of the internet.

Game Modes:

  • 🔍 Feud Mode: Guess the top 4 autocomplete results. Watch out for decoys!
  • 🅰️ Letter Pool: A Jeopardy/Hangman hybrid. Guess the single blank word.
  • 📈 Higher or Lower: Guess which search term gets more traffic!

The Rules:

  • Guess wrong (or hit a decoy), and you get a Strike.
  • 3 Strikes means you lose control!
  • 🚨 The Steal: The next team gets one chance to guess a remaining answer and steal the points!
